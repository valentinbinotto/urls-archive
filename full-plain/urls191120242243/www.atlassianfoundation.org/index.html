Partnering with Room to Read

Atlassian Foundation has partnered with Room to Read for over a decade, in support of creating a world free from illiteracy and gender inequality.

A quarter of Atlassian employees support Room to Read through our Dollars-A-Day giving program - helping to provide education and life skills to more than 740,000 children in 9 countries.

Helping Seabin fill global data gaps to save our oceans

The Seabin Project provides innovative, data driven, tangible solutions to both reduce plastic in our waterways and support new policy and legislation on land.

Atlassian employees helped Seabin set up a system using Jira Service Management to manage its field assets that are collecting microplastics, microfibres and more from waterways in global cities.

The adoption of Jira by Seabin will assist their scaling plan of 100 smarter cities by 2050.
