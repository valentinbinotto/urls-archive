EFF at the World Ethical Data Forum

EFF at the World Ethical Data Forum

Share It Share on Twitter Share on Facebook Copy link
March 17, 2021 - 12:30am PDT to March 19, 2021 - 12:00am PDT

EFF Director of Cybersecurity Eva Galperin and EFF Special Advisor Cory Doctorow are speaking at the World Ethical Data Forum (WEDF), taking place online.

Catch Eva’s fireside chat, or tune into the first screening of a new series featuring her work:

Stalkerware and the Dark Side of Tech
Wednesday, March 17, 2021 12:35 PM to 1:25 PM Pacific

Defenders of Digital: Eva Galperin
Wednesday, March 17, 2021 12:30 PM to 12:40 PM Pacific

Catch Cory’s fireside chat and keynote address:

Privacy, Interoperability & the Rewilding of the Internet
Wednesday, March 17, 2021 1:15 PM to 2:00 PM Pacific

Defining the Future: Philosophy & Science Fiction
Friday, March 19, 2021 1:15 PM to 2:00 PM Pacific

More about the event:

The World Ethical Data Digital Forum is an event that embraces the full range of interrelated issues around the use and future of data — from AI, analytics, and privacy, to press freedoms and political process. The speakers' list consists of highly influential authors, Pulitzer Prize-winning journalists, academics, government officials and business executives as well as representatives from the UN, UNICEF, the Law Society, EFF, GNOME, IBM, Twitter, PwC, Kaspersky, ProtonMail and many more.
